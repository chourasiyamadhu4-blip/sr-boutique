About SR Boutique

👩‍🎨

Our Story

Welcome to SR Boutique, where passion meets precision. We are a family-based boutique inspired by the vision of Sandhya and Ramishwar, two dedicated professionals with decades of experience in tailoring and fashion design.

Our specialty lies in creating custom-designed garments exclusively for women and girls. From elegant suits to stunning lehengas, from intricate blouses to exclusive designer dresses, every piece is crafted with love and attention to detail.

We believe that every woman deserves clothing that makes her feel confident and beautiful. Our skilled designers and tailors work closely with each client to understand their unique style and preferences, bringing their fashion dreams to life.

👩‍💼 Meet Our Founders

Sandhya: Lead Designer with 20+ years of experience in fashion design and custom tailoring.

Ramishwar: Master Tailor with expertise in precision stitching and quality craftsmanship.
